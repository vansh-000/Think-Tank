Getting Started :
This is a quiz game where you can test your knowledge on variours
To get started, simply click on the "Start Quiz" button given below
Choosing a Quiz:
Once you've clicked on the "Play Now" button, you'll be taken to the quiz selection screen.
Choose a quiz category that interests you. We have a wide range of topics available, including geography, history, science, movies, and more.
Answering Questions :
Once you've selected a quiz, you'll start answering questions. Read each question carefully, and select the answer you think is correct.
You may have a time limit for each question, so try to answer quickly but accurately.
Scoring:
You'll earn points for each correct answer. Some quizzes may have bonus points or multipliers for certain questions or difficulty levels.
Leaderboard :
After completing the quiz, you'll see your score and how it compares to other players on the leaderboard.
Compete with friends or other players to see who can achieve the highest score!
